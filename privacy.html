🌙 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Sleep Sound Escape ("we", "our") operates the YouTube channel @SleepSoundEscape and this website. This page explains our privacy practices.

Information We Collect

This website does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any form of user identification.

YouTube

Our YouTube channel is subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. We use the YouTube Data API v3 solely to upload and manage our own videos. We do not collect or store data about viewers.

Pinterest

We share our video content on Pinterest to reach a wider audience. We use the Pinterest API solely to create pins linking to our YouTube videos. We do not collect or store any Pinterest user data. Pinterest's Privacy Policy applies to all interactions on their platform: https://policy.pinterest.com/privacy-policy.

Third-Party Content

All audio used in our videos is CC0 licensed (public domain) sourced from Freesound.org and similar libraries. All visual content is CC0 licensed sourced from Pexels.com. No copyrighted material belonging to third parties is used.

Children's Privacy

Our content is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via the contact form on our YouTube channel page.
